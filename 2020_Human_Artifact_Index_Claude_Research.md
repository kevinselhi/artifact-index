# The 2020 Human Artifact Index
## Claude Code Research Report

**Research Methodology:** WebSearch-based investigation across 11+ professional service sectors
**Date Compiled:** October 24, 2025
**Data Source:** Web searches of industry reports, professional service billing data, and market research

---

## Executive Summary

This index ranks 100 professional artifact types by their typical economic value in calendar year 2020. The research reveals that high-value artifacts concentrate in:
- **Financial Services** (M&A advisory, restructuring, IPOs)
- **Medical/Pharmaceutical** (clinical trials, FDA submissions)
- **Legal Services** (complex litigation, IP, securities)
- **Management Consulting** (strategy, digital transformation)
- **Technology** (custom software, cybersecurity, cloud migration)

### Key Findings:
- **Highest-value artifacts** (>$1M): Clinical trials, FDA submissions, M&A advisory, IPO services
- **Value distribution**: Financial and medical sectors dominate ultra-high-value artifacts
- **2020 COVID-19 impact**: Increased demand for digital transformation, cloud migration, and cybersecurity artifacts
- **Typical range**: Most professional artifacts valued between $25K-$500K
