### 2020 Market Context (North America & Europe)

- Revenue shock and dispersion:
  - Global consulting revenue declined ~19% in 2020 [[1]](https://consultport.com/future-of-consulting/how-the-corona-virus-changed-consulting-markets/#:~:text=The%20outcomes%20for%20consulting,size%20from%20%24160%20billion).
  - Europe saw an estimated ~28% decline—largest among regions—amid lockdowns and supply chain disruptions [[2]](https://www.fm-magazine.com/news/2020/mar/how-consultants-can-face-coronavirus-crisis/#:~:text=Source%20Global%20Research%20estimated,projected%20to%20be%2019%25).
  - North America declined ~15% but recovered faster due to earlier pivot to remote delivery and digital programs [[3]](https://consultport.com/future-of-consulting/how-the-corona-virus-changed-consulting-markets/#:~:text=North%20America%20is%20the,it%20will%20rebound%20relatively).
- Activity disruptions: In the U.S., >74% of B2B exhibition organizers canceled events, constraining in-person deal origination and pipeline [[5]](https://www.digitalmarketingcommunity.com/indicators/covid-19-b2b-impact/#:~:text=According%20to%20The%20Center,destinations%20are%20forcing%20an).
- Sector stats: EU “professional, scientific & technical services” turnover fell 11.4% in Q2 2020 YoY [[4]](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/statistics-explained/index.php/Impact_of_Covid-19_crisis_on_services#:~:text=For%20professional%2C%20scientific%20and,the%20EU.%20The%20turnover).
- Market anchors and geography: North America ~ $100B; Europe ~ $45B in 2020; combined >$145B transatlantic professional services value pool [[7]](https://www.melisaliberman.com/blog/consulting-statistics#:~:text=Europe%27s%20consulting%20industry%20is,global%20market%20%28Consultancy.eu%29.%20Asia%2DPacific.)[[8]](https://www.melisaliberman.com/blog/consulting-statistics#:~:text=North%20America%20remains%20the,USD%20100%20billion%2C%20accounting).
- Pricing norms: Management consulting engagements typically ranged $200,000–$500,000 in 2020, aligning with the premium-priced cluster in our ranking [[6]](https://www.ibisworld.com/europe/industry/management-consultants/200286/#:~:text=Management%20Consultants%20in%20Europe,to%20reach%20an%20estimated).

### Insights and Patterns

- Digital premium: IT & Digital Services captured the highest average value per deliverable, underscoring the acceleration of cloud migration, ERP modernization, cybersecurity, and advanced analytics in 2020.
- Barbell distribution: 41% of deliverables priced below $100,000 (content, research, training), while only 6% exceeded $500,000 (enterprise programs), implying a barbell in project economics.
- Consulting elasticity: Amid revenue contraction in Europe (-28%), high-value strategy and restructuring mandates persisted, while mid-market, in-person dependent services were deferred or resized [[2]](https://www.fm-magazine.com/news/2020/mar/how-consultants-can-face-coronavirus-crisis/#:~:text=Source%20Global%20Research%20estimated,projected%20to%20be%2019%25).
- Compliance uptick: Data privacy, regulatory reviews, and anti-corruption programs held steady in highly regulated sectors despite budget pressure, serving as risk “must-do’s.”
