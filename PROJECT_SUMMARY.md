## üéì Recommendations for Future Research

### Methodological Best Practices

**From Gemini:**
1. Define valuation philosophy upfront and explicitly
2. Use "Data Scarcity Protocol" - be transparent about proxy data
3. Assign confidence levels to estimates
4. Create detailed narrative profiles for top artifacts
5. Use public filings when available (court, SEC)

**From Claude:**
1. Provide consistent detail across all items
2. Use tiered organization for clarity
3. Document methodology transparently (show your work)
4. Cover mid-tier thoroughly (often most actionable)
5. Make research reproducible

**Combined Best Practice:**
> **Use multiple research approaches and be explicit about what you're measuring.**
